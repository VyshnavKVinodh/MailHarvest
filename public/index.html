KEY FEATURES

Built for Serious Lead Generation

Everything you need to extract, validate, and organize email contacts at scale

Built-in Email Validation

Every scraped email can be verified against real mail servers. MailHarvest checks DNS records, tests deliverability, detects catch-all domains, and suggests fixes for invalid addresses.

Smart Two-Phase Crawling

Priority algorithm targets contact, team, and leadership pages first, then scans remaining pages. Quick mode (30 pages) or Deep mode (200 pages) with domain reachability pre-check.

6 Extraction Strategies

Finds emails via mailto links, team sections, profile cards, tables, full-text scan, and phone links. Automatically extracts names, titles, and phone numbers.

Styled Excel Export

Export to .xlsx with purple headers, color-coded validation status, auto-filters, and all contact fields including LinkedIn profile search links.

Multi-Language Support

Detects non-Latin names and designations, translates them to English via Google Translate, and shows both original and translated text side by side.

100% Self-Hosted

Runs entirely on your local machine. No data leaves your network. No API keys needed. No usage limits. Full control over your scraping pipeline.

Tips for Best Results

  • Enter just the domain — no need for https:// or trailing slashes
  • MailHarvest prioritizes About, Team, Contact pages automatically
  • For large batches (10+ domains), scraping may take a few minutes — progress updates live
  • Use Validate Emails after scraping to check which emails are real
  • Invalid emails show smart suggestions for corrections
