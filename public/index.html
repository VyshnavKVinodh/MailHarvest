Smart Contact Extraction Tool

MailHarvest

Extract emails, phone numbers, names, designations & LinkedIn profiles from any website domain — then validate every email for deliverability and export to Excel.

How It Works

1

Add Domains

Type website domains (e.g. example.com) and click Add, or paste a list from Excel. Choose Quick (30 pages) or Deep (200 pages) crawl mode.

2

Scrape & Validate

Click Start Scraping to extract emails, phones, names & designations with live progress. Then validate emails to check deliverability.

3

Export to Excel

Select contacts, then Download Excel to get a formatted .xlsx with Name, Designation, Phone, Email, Validation, LinkedIn, and more.

Built-in Email Validation

Every scraped email is verified against real mail servers — DNS MX lookup, SMTP deliverability check, catch-all domain detection, and smart fix suggestions for invalid addresses (80+ known typos).

Phone & LinkedIn Discovery

Automatically extracts phone numbers from tel: links and page context. Generates LinkedIn profile search links for every named contact. Contacts with only a phone (no email) are still captured.

Smart Two-Phase Crawling

Phase 1 targets high-value pages (Contact, Team, Leadership). Phase 2 crawls remaining pages. Non-English names and designations are auto-translated to English.

💡 Tips for Best Results

  • Enter just the domain — no need for https:// or trailing slashes
  • Paste multiple domains from Excel — they're added automatically
  • Use Quick mode (30 pages) on Vercel, Deep mode (200 pages) when running locally
  • MailHarvest prioritizes About, Team, Contact, Leadership pages automatically
  • Use Validate Emails after scraping to check which emails are real
  • Filter results by validation status, name, phone, or designation
  • Invalid emails show smart suggestions — typo corrections and similar domain hints
